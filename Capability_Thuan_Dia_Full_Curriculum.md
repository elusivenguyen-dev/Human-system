# Capability: Thuận Địa (Aligning with Structure)
## Full Curriculum — 8 Levels

> "Thuận Địa không phải là 'thuận theo hoàn cảnh'. Mà là năng lực nhìn thấy: Môi Trường → Cấu Trúc → Ràng Buộc → Động Lực → Hành Vi → Kết Quả."
> 
> Nguyên lý trung tâm: Structure → Behavior. Hành vi không xuất hiện ngẫu nhiên. Hành vi xuất hiện từ cấu trúc.

---

# Level 1 — Structural Awareness

## 1. Systems Thinking Foundations

**Học:**
- System boundaries (what is inside/outside?)
- Inputs–Outputs (what flows in/out?)
- Stocks & Flows (accumulation and movement)
- Feedback Loops (reinforcing and balancing)

**Nguồn:**
- Donella Meadows — *Thinking in Systems*
- Peter Senge — *The Fifth Discipline*

**OS Mapping:** L3 Mental Models (Feedback Interpretation), L12 Reverse Causality (Structural Diagnosis), L6 Physics (Dynamic Systems Thinking)

**Key Insight:** Con người nhìn thấy events. Systems thinkers nhìn thấy structure tạo ra events.

---

## 2. Structure Creates Behavior

**Năng lực:**
Nhìn thấy:
```text
Structure → Behavior → Results
```
Thay vì:
```text
People → Results
```

**OS Mapping:** L12 Reverse Causality (Outcome → Structure), L13 Human Systems (Architecture), L6 Physics (Constraint Modeling)

**Key Insight:** Kết quả xấu không phải do người xấu. Kết quả xấu thường do cấu trúc tạo ra hành vi xấu.

---

## 3. Environmental Scanning

**Học:**
- Physical environment (geography, climate, infrastructure)
- Economic environment (market, capital, labor)
- Technology environment (disruption, enablement)
- Institutional environment (laws, norms, culture)

**Câu hỏi:**
> "Địa hình hệ thống này là gì?"

**OS Mapping:** L14 Strategic (Thuận Địa), L3 Mental Models (Scale Awareness), L6 Physics (Constraint Modeling)

**Key Insight:** Không thể thay đổi hành vi nếu không hiểu môi trường cho phép/ không cho phép điều gì.

---

# Level 2 — Constraint Intelligence

## 4. Constraint Analysis

**Học:**
- Resource constraints (what is scarce?)
- Information constraints (what is unknown?)
- Capability constraints (what cannot be done?)
- Governance constraints (what is not allowed?)

**Nguồn cảm hứng:** Eliyahu Goldratt — *The Goal* (Theory of Constraints)

**OS Mapping:** L6 Physics (Constraint Recognition), L12 Reverse Causality (Structural Limits), L14 Strategic (Thuận Địa)

**Key Insight:** Constraints không phải enemy. Constraints là reality. Innovation = working within constraints, not ignoring them.

---

## 5. Resource Architecture

**Năng lực:**
```text
Resources → Capabilities → Performance
```

**Học:**
- Capital (financial, human, social)
- Talent (skills, knowledge, experience)
- Technology (tools, platforms, infrastructure)
- Data (information, analytics, insight)
- Relationships (networks, trust, access)

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Resource Control), L14 Strategic (Tài/Cốt), L8 Resistance (Energy)

**Key Insight:** Resources không tự tạo value. Value = resources × architecture × execution.

---

## 6. Dependency Mapping

**Học:**
- Critical dependencies (what cannot fail?)
- Single points of failure (where is fragility?)
- Ecosystem dependencies (who do we depend on? who depends on us?)
- Cascade effects (if X fails, what happens to Y, Z?)

**OS Mapping:** L3 Mental Models (Causal Modeling), L12 Reverse Causality (Fault Tree), L6 Physics (Dynamic Systems)

**Key Insight:** Hệ thống không sụp đổ vì mọi thứ đều yếu. Hệ thống sụp đổ vì một điểm yếu duy nhất.

---

# Level 3 — Structural Diagnostics

## 7. Reverse Structure Analysis

**Đi từ:**
```text
Outcome
      ↑
Behavior
      ↑
Incentives
      ↑
Structure
```

**OS Mapping:** L12 Reverse Causality (Full Architecture), L13 Human Systems (Diagnostics), L3 Mental Models (Causal Modeling)

**Key Insight:** Truy ngược từ kết quả đến cấu trúc = nhìn thấy cái khuôn tạo ra hình.

---

## 8. Organizational Diagnostics

**Học:**
- Governance diagnostics (who decides? how?)
- Capability diagnostics (what can we do? what can't we?)
- Incentive diagnostics (what is rewarded? punished? ignored?)
- Culture diagnostics (what memes dominate?)

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (18 modules), L12 Reverse Causality (Level 4 Structural Diagnosis), L1 Motivation Architecture

**Key Insight:** Tổ chức không phải những gì được viết trong handbook. Tổ chức là những gì được thưởng và phạt.

---

## 9. Failure Mode Analysis

**Học:**
- Failure patterns (what fails repeatedly? why?)
- Collapse mechanisms (how do systems die?)
- Cascading failures (one failure triggers another)
- Early warning signals (what indicates impending collapse?)

**Mục tiêu:**
Nhìn thấy nơi cấu trúc sẽ gãy **trước khi** nó gãy.

**OS Mapping:** L6 Physics (Entropy Awareness), L8 Resistance (Collapse Probability), L12 Reverse Causality (Collapse Analysis)

**Key Insight:** Hệ thống không sụp đổ bất ngờ. Chúng sụp đổ sau khi đã gửi nhiều tín hiệu mà không ai đọc.

---

# Level 4 — Institutional Structure

## 10. Institutional Economics

**Nghiên cứu:**
- Rules of the game (formal and informal)
- Transaction costs (cost of coordination)
- Incentive systems (what drives behavior?)
- Path dependence (history matters)

**Tác giả:** Douglass North — *Institutions, Institutional Change and Economic Performance*

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Governance Architecture), L12 Reverse Causality (Incentive Analysis), L6 Physics (Constraint Modeling)

**Key Insight:** Institutions không phải buildings. Institutions là rules — và rules determine behavior.

---

## 11. Governance Architecture

**Học:**
```text
Power → Rules → Incentives → Behavior → Outcomes
```

**Nghiên cứu:**
- Who has power? (formal vs. informal)
- What are the rules? (written vs. unwritten)
- What do incentives produce? (intended vs. unintended)
- What behaviors emerge? (compliance vs. gaming)

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Power Systems, Governance), L4 Status Hierarchies, L1 Motivation Architecture

**Key Insight:** Governance không phải org chart. Governance là cách quyền lực thực sự được phân phối và sử dụng.

---

## 12. Decision Architecture

**Năng lực:**
Nhìn thấy:
- Ai quyết định? (decision rights)
- Ai ảnh hưởng? (influence networks)
- Ai phủ quyết? (veto power)
- Ai chịu trách nhiệm? (accountability)

**OS Mapping:** L5 Execution OS (Decision), L13 Human Systems (Executive Psychology), L12 Reverse Causality (Decision Reconstruction)

**Key Insight:** Quyết định kém không phải do người kém. Quyết định kém thường do architecture không rõ ràng.

---

# Level 5 — Organizational Structure

## 13. Organization Design

**Nghiên cứu:**
- Functional structure (by function: marketing, sales, ops)
- Divisional structure (by product/region/customer)
- Matrix structure (dual reporting)
- Network structure (distributed, agile)
- Platform structure (ecosystem orchestrator)

**Nguồn:** Jay Galbraith — *Designing Organizations*

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Organization Design), L14 Strategic (Thành Vị), L3 Mental Models (Abstraction)

**Key Insight:** Structure không phải neutral. Mỗi structure tạo ra behavior khác nhau.

---

## 14. Operating Model Design

**Học:**
- Value delivery (how do we create value?)
- Service model (how do we serve?)
- Capability model (what must we be great at?)
- Process architecture (how does work flow?)

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Operating Model), L5 Execution OS (Process), L12 Reverse Causality (Behavioral Trace)

**Key Insight:** Operating model = how strategy becomes reality. Không có operating model = strategy là wishful thinking.

---

## 15. Enterprise Architecture

**Khung:**
- The Open Group TOGAF
- ArchiMate

**Học:**
```text
Strategy → Capabilities → Processes → Technology
```

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Enterprise Architecture), L3 Mental Models (Multi-Model Integration), L6 Physics (Scale Awareness)

**Key Insight:** Enterprise Architecture = bridge giữa strategy và execution. Không có EA = strategy và IT nói chuyện khác ngôn ngữ.

---

# Level 6 — Ecosystem Structure

## 16. Ecosystem Thinking

**Học:**
- Ecosystem mapping (who is in? who is out?)
- Network effects (value increases with scale)
- Value systems (who captures value? who creates?)
- Complementarity (who needs whom?)

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Ecosystem), L14 Strategic (Hòa), L3 Mental Models (Scale Awareness)

**Key Insight:** Không có tổ chức nào tồn tại độc lập. Mỗi tổ chức là node trong ecosystem.

---

## 17. Platform Dynamics

**Nghiên cứu:**
- Multi-sided markets (connecting producers and consumers)
- Platform governance (who decides rules?)
- Ecosystem incentives (how do participants benefit?)
- Network effects (direct, indirect, cross-side)

**OS Mapping:** L13 Human Systems (Platform), L1 Motivation Architecture (Incentive Design), L6 Physics (Dynamic Systems)

**Key Insight:** Platform không phải product. Platform = ecosystem orchestration.

---

## 18. Complex Adaptive Systems

**Nguồn:** Santa Fe Institute

**Học:**
- Emergence (system properties > sum of parts)
- Adaptation (learning, evolving, self-organizing)
- Self-organization (order from chaos, no central control)
- Phase transitions (sudden shifts, tipping points)

**OS Mapping:** L6 Physics (Dynamic Systems, Nonlinear Thresholds), L8 Resistance (Collapse Probability), L3 Mental Models (Feedback Interpretation)

**Key Insight:** Complex systems không thể được controlled. Chúng chỉ có thể được influenced, nudged, và adapted to.

---

# Level 7 — Structural Renewal

## 19. Structural Death Recognition

**Năng lực:**
Nhìn thấy:
- Cấu trúc suy tàn (decay signals)
- Governance erosion (rules no longer enforced)
- Capability decay (skills atrophy, technology obsolete)
- Information distortion (truth no longer flows)

**OS Mapping:** L6 Physics (Entropy Awareness), L8 Resistance (Degradation), L12 Reverse Causality (Collapse Analysis)

**Key Insight:** Hệ thống chết không phải vì bị tấn công. Hệ thống chết vì entropy — không bảo trì, không adapt.

---

## 20. Renewal Architecture

**Học:**
- Structural redesign (what must change?)
- Governance redesign (who decides what?)
- Capability redesign (what must we learn/unlearn?)
- Incentive redesign (what must be rewarded?)

**OS Mapping:** L11 Internal Reverse (Structural Renewal), L13 Human Systems (Adaptation), L5 Spirit (Suffering Integration)

**Key Insight:** Renewal không phải repair. Renewal = redesign from first principles.

---

## 21. Turnaround Systems

**Năng lực:**
```text
Decline → Diagnosis → Stabilization → Renewal → Growth
```

**Học:**
- Crisis management (stop the bleeding)
- Stabilization (preserve what works)
- Core renewal (rebuild foundations)
- Growth architecture (scale what matters)

**OS Mapping:** L12 Reverse Causality (Turnaround), L13 Human Systems (Renewal), L14 Strategic (Biết Thời)

**Key Insight:** Turnaround không phải về optimism. Turnaround = về realism + discipline + timing.

---

# Level 8 — Strategic Geography

## 22. Economic Geography

**Học:**
- Capital flows (where does money go? why?)
- Supply chains (who makes what? where?)
- Industrial clusters (why do industries cluster?)
- Regional advantages (what makes a place special?)

**OS Mapping:** L14 Strategic (Thuận Địa), L6 Physics (Scale Awareness), L3 Mental Models (Abstraction)

**Key Insight:** Geography không phải destiny. Nhưng geography là constraint — và constraints shape strategy.

---

## 23. Geoeconomics

**Nghiên cứu:**
```text
Geography → Resources → Power → Trade → Prosperity
```

**Học:**
- Resource geography (oil, water, minerals, land)
- Trade routes (chokepoints, corridors, hubs)
- Geopolitical competition (who controls what?)
- Economic statecraft (sanctions, aid, investment)

**OS Mapping:** L14 Strategic (Ứng Thiên), L6 Physics (Constraint Modeling), L13 Human Systems (Power Systems)

**Key Insight:** Geoeconomics = economics + geography + power. Không thể tách rời.

---

## 24. Civilizational Structures

**Học:**
- Institutional evolution (how do institutions change over centuries?)
- State capacity (what can a state do?)
- Infrastructure systems (roads, ports, energy, digital)
- Long-cycle development (rise, peak, decline, renewal)

**OS Mapping:** L14 Strategic (Đại), L13 Human Systems (Civilizational), L6 Physics (Entropy, Scale)

**Key Insight:** Civilizations không sụp đổ vì một sự kiện. Chúng sụp đổ vì institutional decay + inability to adapt.

# Integration with Strategic Architecture

Trong chuỗi:
```text
Hiểu Nhân → Thuận Địa → Ứng Thiên → Biết Thời → Thành Vị
```

Đó là bước chuyển từ tư duy tâm lý sang tư duy cấu trúc.
